Wings of Fire Book 6: Moon Rising

Helloo Wings fo Fire readers! My name is Pranshu Dash. In this website, I have reveiwed Wings of Fire Book 6: Moon Rising. This series is great, and you should really read it if you are into fantady and adventure!

Introduction

This book series revolves around a number of prophecies that are given to young dragons(dragonets). Every series has a new prophecy, and each series has 5 books. There is a decent amount of violence, the first book being tho most. You should only read this series if you are at least in grade 4. There is some amount of comedy, which I think is a very good thing.This book specifically shows the how the dragons are slowly understanding each others religion, wihtout discrimination or wars. And obviously, a lot of adventure. A lot. So leas dive right into the reveiw!
